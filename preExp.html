Intro

Cedibility Judgments in Search Engine Result Pages

Thank you for participating in our study about credibility judgments!
We conduct this study within the class "Informationverhalten verstehen".

In the following, you will get 6 pages with 8 results each and your task is
to make an assumption about the credibility of the websites, which are represented by a snippet.

This is followed by a short post-questionnaire where you have to provide some demographic information
and questions regarding the given search results.

The whole study takes approximately 20 minutes to complete.

If you are a Medieninformatik- or Informationswissenschaft-Student at the University of Regensburg,
you can get 0.5 VP-hours for this. To receive them, just enter your email and id at the end of the questionary.

Your data is only seen by the study conductors and supervisors and is later analyzed in a paper.
Records are automatically deleted after the end of the research.
