A study about the credibility of web search engines

Thank you for participating in our study about credibility judgments. We conduct this study within the class "Informationverhalten verstehen".

In the following, you will get 6 pages with 8 results each and your task is to make an assumption about the credibility of the websites, which are represented by a snippet. This is followed by a short post-questionnaire where you have to provide some demographic information and questions regarding the given search results. The whole study takes approximately XX minutes to complete.

If you are from Medieninformatik or Informationswissenschaft at the University of Regensburg, you can get X VP-hours for this. To receive them, just enter your email and id at the end of the questionary.

We will not identify you by your real name in any reports using information obtained from this online study and that your confidentiality as a participant in this online study will remain secure. It is only seen by us study conductors and supervisors and is later analyzed in a paper. Records are automatically deleted after the end of the research.
